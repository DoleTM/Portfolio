About Me

From the beginning of my professional career, I instantly recognized that I had a passion for continual learning. I soon began to take the initiative and became fascinated with improving efficiency in projects bigger than myself. It was here where I honed in on my troubleshooting skills and the ability to work under pressure. Now I have taken the leap into coding to pursue my internal passion for continual learning and the chance to make something through code that would improve efficiency in people's lives.
